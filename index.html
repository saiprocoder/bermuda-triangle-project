Bermuda Triangle



Bermuda Triangle is a mysterious area where aeroplanes and ships have vanished. This is a triang'e in the Atlantic Ocean. As you know, triangle has 3 corners. The three corners of the Bermuda Triangle are Bermuda (Island), Miami and Puerto Rico. The gravitional pull is so strong that it can pull any ship which is 5 km away from the side. In this triangle, compasses stop working. We cannot establish connection to any airbase. There are theories that there is a sea monster and sky monster that gobbles up ships and aeroplanes.
